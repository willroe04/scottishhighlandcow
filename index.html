About Highland Cows

Scottish Highland cows, or Highland Cattle, are a hardy breed known for their long horns and long, wavy coats. They are well adapted to the rugged terrain and harsh climate of the Scottish Highlands.

Fun Facts

  • Highland cows have a double coat that keeps them warm in cold weather.
  • They are known for their gentle and friendly temperament.
  • Highland cattle can live for up to 15 years or more.
  • Their long horns can grow up to 3 feet in length!
